## AUTO INSURANCE

**Service Page:** /services/auto-insurance
- Title: Auto Insurance in Alabama & Georgia | Coffey Agencies
- H1: Auto Insurance in Alabama & Georgia
- Primary Keyword Target: "auto insurance Alabama Georgia" / "auto insurance quote"
- Has FAQ: Yes (6 questions)

**Learn Page:** /learn/auto-insurance-guide
- Title: Auto Insurance in Alabama & Georgia: Complete Guide | Coffey Agencies
- H1: Auto Insurance in Alabama & Georgia: What You Actually Need to Know
- Primary Keyword Target: "auto insurance guide" / "auto insurance Alabama Georgia guide"
- Has FAQ: Yes (6 questions)

**Cannibalization Risk:** MEDIUM

**Issues Found:**
- Title tags are very similar — both start with "Auto Insurance in Alabama & Georgia"
- H1 headings are nearly identical — both use "Auto Insurance in Alabama & Georgia" as the primary phrase
- Meta descriptions overlap significantly — both mention "Alabama and Georgia" and "coverage"
- Both pages target the same primary keyword phrase "auto insurance Alabama Georgia"
- FAQ count is identical (6 questions each), though questions differ

**Recommendations:**
- Service page title should emphasize transactional intent: "Auto Insurance Quotes in Alabama & Georgia | Get Coverage Today"
- Learn page title is acceptable but could be more distinct: "Complete Auto Insurance Guide: Alabama & Georgia Requirements & Coverage"
- Service page H1 should focus on action: "Get Auto Insurance Coverage in Alabama & Georgia"
- Learn page H1 is fine but could emphasize education: "Auto Insurance Guide: Everything You Need to Know About Coverage in Alabama & Georgia"
- Ensure FAQs don't overlap — service page should focus on "how to get coverage" while learn page focuses on "what is coverage"

**Cross-linking Status:**
- Service → Learn: No (missing link to /learn/auto-insurance-guide)
- Learn → Service: Yes (links to /services/auto-insurance via internalLinks)
